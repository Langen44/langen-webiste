The powerplant

A Maranello-born
two-stroke V-twin

Developed by Vincenzo Mattia at Vins Motors in Maranello, the 249.5cc 90° V-twin is fed by Langen's own electronic fuel injection with two port injectors, computer-controlled oil injection, electronically manipulated exhaust valves, and carbon fibre reeds. Hundreds of hours on the dyno have produced a powerband that defies everything you know about two-strokes — smooth, exploitable mid-range with a manic top-end that delivers 76 horsepower at 11,700 rpm.

Chassis & suspension

Lightweight
precision

Built around a custom-specified 7020T6 aluminium space frame — laser cut, 3D bent and immaculately welded — with bonded connection points inspired by aerospace construction.

Front suspension
Öhlins 43mm right-way-up forks. Compression, rebound damping and spring preload adjustment.
Rear suspension
Twin bespoke Ktech Piggyback Razors. Fully adjustable compression and rebound damping.
Braking
320mm dual discs with 4-piston radial calipers. Race-proven stopping power at just 112 kg.
Craftsmanship

Every detail
considered

01
Carbon fibre bodywork
Hand-laid carbon fibre fuel tank and bodywork using a special resin, created in the Vins Motors autoclave in Maranello. The 3.7-gallon tank includes a separate 0.26-gallon oil reservoir — both lined using specialist composite techniques developed for high-performance motorsport.
02
CNC-machined components
Precision-machined aluminium and titanium throughout — from triple clamps to footrest hangers. Every fastener, bracket and mount is engineered to save weight without compromise. British craftsmanship at every level of detail.
03
Bespoke to the rider
Every Two Stroke is built to measure. Handlebar type and positioning, seat height, footrest location, rear suspension tuning, paint, chassis finish — each tailored to the individual owner's dimensions and preferences.
