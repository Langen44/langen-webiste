How we work

The Langen
process

Every Langen is built to individual order. There is no production line, no showroom floor, no compromise. There is a process — and it begins the moment you make contact.

01
First contact
You reach out. We listen. A conversation with Christofer and the team — not a sales pitch, but a genuine discussion about what you want from your motorcycle.
02
Visit Wigan
Come to the factory. See the bikes being built. Meet the team. Sit on the frame. Understand what makes a Langen different before committing to one.
03
Specification
Your motorcycle is specified in full — colour, finish, suspension setup, ergonomics. Every decision made together. Nothing left to chance.
04
The build
Your motorcycle is built by hand in Wigan. We'll keep you informed throughout. When it's ready, you'll know — because we'll have tested it ourselves first.
05
Delivery
Personal delivery. A handover briefing. A direct line to the people who built it. The relationship doesn't end at delivery — it starts there.
Why Langen

The principles we
build by

Obsessive engineering
We question every component. Every gram of weight, every material choice, every fastener. Not because the spec sheet demands it — because the rider deserves it.
Genuinely handbuilt
Langen motorcycles are built one at a time, by hand, in Wigan. The highest UK-local manufacturing content of any motorcycle produced in Britain today.
Built for the individual
No two Langens are alike. Every motorcycle is specified, built and delivered for a specific person. Your geometry. Your finish. Your ride.
Made in Britain
Built at Kingfisher Court, Wigan, by a team with backgrounds at CCM, Triumph, BAC Mono, McLaren and M-Sport. British engineering at its most focused.
