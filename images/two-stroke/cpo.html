Why CPO

The Langen CPO
programme

01
Factory inspection
Every CPO motorcycle undergoes a rigorous 40+ point inspection before handover. Completed by the factory technician who knows your specific motorcycle inside out.

02
Factory-backed warranty
The warranty is provided directly by Langen Motorcycles — not a third party. Every CPO motorcycle comes with a minimum 12 months warranty. The same team, the same commitment, the same direct line to the people who built it.
03
Certified vehicle history
A detailed background on every motorcycle — previous ownership, service records, any modifications, race use, storage history. Our in-house database combined with external data suppliers provides the complete picture. No gaps, no surprises.
04
Motorcycle provenance
CPO Langens command a premium over non-certified pre-owned examples. We built each one by hand and know them intimately. A new Certificate of Ownership is issued from the factory with every CPO sale.
05
Re-specification of the motorcycle
This is where Langen differs entirely from any other pre-owned programme. When you buy a used motorcycle from us, we offer you the opportunity to work with our design team and customise it to your exact requirements — whether that's a change of colour, new wheels, revised ergonomics, or a full specification update. The motorcycle becomes yours in every sense before you take delivery.

No other manufacturer offers this. It's the reason pre-owned Langens are genuinely desirable rather than merely available.
