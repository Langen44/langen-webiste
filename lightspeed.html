Powertrain

Built
by Buell

The Lightspeed is powered by a bespoke 1,190cc 72° Rotax V-twin developed in partnership with Buell — a powerplant chosen not for displacement alone, but for its character. Deep, muscular torque from low in the rev range builds to 185 horsepower with an urgency that rewards precision and punishes hesitation. This is an engine that communicates. Every throttle opening is a direct conversation between rider and machine.

In-house electronics suite

The intelligence
inside

Every Lightspeed ships with a full electronic suite designed and developed in-house at Langen — not adapted from an existing platform, but built from the ground up for this motorcycle.

01
Traction Control
Multi-mode system calibrated for road and track. Intervenes with surgical precision — invisible when not needed, decisive when it is.
02
Launch Control
Optimises power delivery from a standing start. Eliminates wheel-spin while preserving the raw sensation of 185 horsepower leaving the line.
03
Wheelie Control
Monitors and manages front-wheel lift in real time. Configurable for riders who want to manage it themselves.
04
Cruise Control
Refined cruise management for long-distance capability. The Lightspeed is not a track-only machine — it is built to be ridden.
05
ABS
Cornering-capable ABS via HEL stainless braided lines and EBC compound. Balanced feel with maximum stopping confidence.
06
Smiths Dashboard
A bespoke instrument cluster produced by Smiths of England. Analogue clarity meets digital precision — designed exclusively for the Lightspeed.
Chassis & Suspension

Steel trellis.
Carbon skin.

A hand-welded chromoly steel trellis frame — chosen for its feel and tuneability. Carbon fibre bodywork wraps the architecture, delivering mass reduction without sacrificing the rigidity the chassis demands.

Front suspension
Öhlins fully adjustable forks — compression, rebound and spring preload individually configured
Rear suspension
Öhlins piggyback remote reservoir shock — fully adjustable, set for the individual rider
Brakes
HEL stainless braided lines · EBC compound · Cornering-capable ABS

Your build journey
There is no dealership network, no sales team, no intermediary. From first conversation to final handover, every Lightspeed owner works directly with Christofer and the build team at Wigan. Your motorcycle is specified, constructed and delivered by the same people — and they know it by heart.
UK deliveries Summer 2026
UK-homologated Lightspeeds begin leaving Wigan in Summer 2026. Global homologation — USA, Japan, Middle East, EU and Australia — follows from January 2027. Build slots are allocated in order of reservation.
Langen aftercare
Ownership does not end at delivery. Every Lightspeed is backed by the full Langen aftercare programme — direct, ongoing access to the engineers who built your motorcycle, for as long as you own it.
